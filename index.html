  • About Us

    Who are we?

    For us, training is not a job, it is our calling and passion. We are a team of certified management trainers and e-Learning content developers who can take care of your training needs. We use storytelling techniques to build engaging training videos, induction modules, and we create immersive videos or slides to communicate your vision, values, and codes of conduct.

    What do we do?

    We conduct online/face-to-face training in soft skills, leadership skills, training for first time managers, prevention of sexual harrasment, and any other executive training program. We help you to publish your instructor led training programs as self-based e-Learning modules. We transform text based training slides into story based animated videos with in-built games and quizes.
