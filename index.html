    What Services Do I Offer?

    There are three basic stages in creating an online training, where I can offer my specialised services.
    Stage 1: You need content that addresses the training needs of both the employees and the organisation. Get in touch with me to create relevant and contemporary learning content for training your executives.
    Stage 2: The content needs to be designed to produce a lucid and free flowing learning experience for the learners, which will motivate them to complete the course and absorb the intended learning objectives. I can help you to publish your instructor led training programs as self-based e-Learning modules. I transform text based training slides into story based animated videos with in-built games and quizes. This is my instructional design service. So, what is instructional design? You need to answer a few questions in this stage of course creation, which instructional designers can answer:

    1. How can we make our course less complex, without compromising on the learning outcomes?
    2. Do we need visuals? If yes, then what kind of visuals will facilitate the learning process?
    3. Should the course be video based?
    4. Can we use animated videos?
    5. How much of gamification and interactivity should be included?

    Stage 3: Finally, our production process will use the storyboard created in stage 2 to deliver the complete online course. My preferred authoring tools are Vyond, Articulate 360, Canva and MS PowerPoint. I conduct online/face-to-face training in soft skills, leadership skills, training for first time managers, prevention of sexual harrasment, and any other executive training program.
